Logical Model: Patient (CDA Class)

Official URL: http://hl7.org/cda/stds/core/StructureDefinition/Patient Version: 2.0.0-sd-202406-matchbox-patch
Active as of 2024-06-19 Computable Name: Patient

A LivingSubject as a recipient of health care services from a healthcare provider. The patient is the player; the provider is the scoper.

Usage:

  • Use this Logical Model Profile: PatientRole (CDA Class)
